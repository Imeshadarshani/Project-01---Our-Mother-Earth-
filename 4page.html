Why Earth is called Blue planet?

Planet earth has been called the "blue planet" due to the abundant water on its surface.(71% of earth is covered by water.)The third closest planet to the sun is the earth. The planet looks blue from outer space since two-thirds of the surface is covered with water, so it is called a blue planet.


The Three layers of Earth

Earth can be divided into three main layers.They are,

  1. Crust
  2. Metal
  3. Core

      4. The core, the mantle and the crust. Each of these layers can be further divided into two parts: the inner and outer core, the upper and lower mantle and the continental and oceanic crust. Both the inner and outer core are made up of mostly iron and a little bit of nickel.
